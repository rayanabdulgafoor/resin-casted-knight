## Overview

This project focused on the design and fabrication of a resin-casted knight chess piece using a combination of digital manufacturing and mold-making techniques. The workflow involved CAD modeling, CAM preparation, CNC machining, silicone mold fabrication, and final resin casting.

![cam](/assets/resin-casted-poster.png)

The project was developed to explore precision fabrication workflows and manufacturing techniques commonly used in prototyping, product development, and low-volume production applications.



---

## Key Features

* Custom-designed knight chess piece
* CAD/CAM workflow development in Fusion 360
* CNC machining using machinable wax
* Silicone mold fabrication
* Resin casting process integration
* Precision surface detailing and replication
* Multi-stage digital fabrication workflow

## Design & Manufacturing Workflow

The fabrication workflow consisted of:

1. CAD modeling of the knight chess piece in Fusion 360
2. CAM toolpath preparation for CNC machining
3. CNC milling of machinable wax using TRAK DPM RX2
4. Silicone mold fabrication
5. Final resin casting and finishing process



---

## CAD & CAM Development

* Designed detailed 3D geometry in Fusion 360
* Developed machining strategies and CAM toolpaths
* Optimized geometry for mold release and manufacturability
* Prepared fabrication-ready machining workflows

## CNC Machining

The master model was machined from machinable wax using a TRAK DPM RX2 CNC bed mill.

![machine](/assets/trak_front_full_pic.jpg)

The machining process involved:

* Roughing and finishing toolpaths
* Surface detail preservation
* Dimensional accuracy validation
* Iterative machining parameter refinement

---

## Mold Making & Resin Casting

* Fabricated a silicone mold from the CNC-machined wax master
* Performed resin casting for final part replication
* Evaluated surface finish and geometric detail transfer
* Explored repeatable low-volume fabrication workflows
